Ship sections

A ship in Nova Protocol is not a monolithic model - it is a root entity with a handful of section children. Each section is mounted to the hull, carries its own mass and health, and contributes exactly one behavior to the whole ship. Knock a section off and the ship loses that capability but keeps flying on whatever is left, which is what makes damage feel local: shoot the turret off and it stops shooting; take out the controller and it can no longer steer.
