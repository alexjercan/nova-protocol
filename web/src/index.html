What you do in Nova Protocol

Assemble a ship, take it into a scenario, and fly it for real - every autopilot maneuver moves the hull and burns the thrusters you bolted on.

01

Modular ship editor

Bolt together hull, controller, thruster, turret and torpedo-bay sections. Each section carries its own mass and health and contributes its behavior to the whole ship.

02

Diegetic autopilot

GOTO, ORBIT and STOP verbs fly the ship through its real actuators - you watch the hull swing and the plume light up. Any manual input takes back control instantly.

03

Gravity wells

Large asteroids pull ships and torpedoes with real inverse-square gravity. Park into a stable circular orbit around the dominant well with a single key.

04

Locks & turrets

Hold CTRL to sweep the radar and lock what you are looking at; turrets compute intercept lead and paint aim pips, while torpedo bays fire guided, blast-damage warheads.

05

Combat you can feel

Trauma camera shake, expanding hit rings and distance-attenuated audio sell every impact and section that gets blown off a hull.

06

Cinematic HUD

Cycle the HUD from full chrome to instruments-only to a clean screen for capturing shots - every widget knows its tier.
