News

News from building Nova Protocol

One post per release, in the spirit of Factorio's Friday Facts - candid about what shipped and why, with the highlights, screenshots, and the occasional video. Patch releases fold into their feature post; for the terse, complete list of every version see CHANGELOG.md.

    2026-07-16 // v0.6.0

    v0.6.0 - Mods over the wire

    A static mod portal and an in-game Explore online tab install and update mods over the wire, dependencies resolve end to end, a Scenarios picker lands on the main menu, and particles return to the web on WebGPU.

  • Thumbnail assets/thumb-news-0.5.0.png
    2026-07-13 // v0.5.0 (+ 0.5.1, 0.5.2)

    v0.5.0 - Lock, look, and a front door

    Passive targeting gives way to deliberate radar locking with a live viewfinder and kill cam, the Shakedown Run teaches the first minutes, typed damage arrives, and the game gets a menu and a home on the web.

  • Thumbnail assets/thumb-news-0.4.0.png
    2026-07-10 // v0.4.0 (+ 0.4.1)

    v0.4.0 - The flight toy learns to fight

    Guided torpedoes, a targeting arc, turret auto-aim, a faction model, an AI wave that fights back, and the first audio and combat juice.

  • Thumbnail assets/thumb-news-0.3.0.png
    2025-11-29 // v0.3.0 (+ 0.3.1)

    v0.3.0 - Where and when it hurts

    Zone lifecycle events for richer scenarios, the torpedo bay with area-of-effect blast damage, per-section health, and sharper shaders - then the Bevy 0.19 jump in 0.3.1.

  • 2025-11-08 // v0.2.0 (+ 0.2.1)

    v0.2.0 - A reason to fly

    A data-driven events and queue system, the first scenario and modding capabilities, and procedurally destructible asteroids. Video devlog included.

  • 2025-10-21 // v0.1.0

    v0.1.0 - A cube learns to fly

    Where it started: thruster-driven modular ships, PD-controlled steering, turrets that shoot, and sections that shatter into chunks. Video devlog included.
