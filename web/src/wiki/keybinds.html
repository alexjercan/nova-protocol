Keybinds

The full control reference. Thrust is analog on the gamepad and on/off on the keyboard. The autopilot verbs each fly a whole maneuver and hand control back; any manual input disengages them immediately. New players should start with the tutorial, which teaches these one at a time.

Weapons

Fire turrets (default)
Launch torpedo Raise weapons, hold a red combat lock, then fire the torpedo bay

Weapon sections are rebindable: in the editor, click a section to bind it to any key or mouse button, so a ship's turret and torpedo controls are whatever its build assigns (the shipped ship fires turrets on the left mouse button).
