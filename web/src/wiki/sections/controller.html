Controller

The controller is the ship's steering system: a proportional-derivative (PD) attitude controller that rotates the ship toward a target heading. It is required for any ship a player or AI can fly.

With no live controller, the hull cannot steer itself - so destroying the controller disables a ship without destroying it outright, leaving a drifting, tumbling wreck. It is also what the autopilot verbs drive when they fly the ship for you.
