How to play

Tutorial // getting off the launch pad

Nova Protocol is a build-and-fly space shooter. You assemble a ship out of modular sections and then take it into a scenario - an asteroid field with gravity wells and hostile ships. The flight computer can fly for you through real thrusters, but the moment you touch a control you take over.

The game loop

The game boots into a main menu with four options:

  • New Game - drops you straight into the asteroid-field scenario with a ready-made player ship.
  • Sandbox - opens the ship editor so you can build a ship and test-fly it in a practice scenario.
  • Settings - placeholder for now.
  • Exit - quits (hidden in the browser build).

In Sandbox you build a ship from sections, then fly it; pressing F1 takes you back to the editor (Sandbox only). In any scenario, Esc pauses the game and gives you Resume / Back to Main Menu / Exit.

Flight controls

Thrust is analog on a gamepad trigger and on/off on the keyboard. The autopilot verbs each fly a whole maneuver and then hand control back; any manual input disengages them immediately.

Main thruster burn W / Space  or  RT
GOTO - fly to the current lock G  or  North button
ORBIT - park into orbit around a gravity well O  or  D-Pad Down
STOP - face retrograde and burn to rest X  or  East button
CANCEL autopilot (resume manual) Z  or  West button

Targeting and camera

Aim / look Mouse motion  or  Right stick
Free look Alt (hold)  or  LT
Combat / turret mode Right mouse button  or  LT2
Cycle locked ship Ctrl + scroll  or  D-Pad Up
Cycle component [ / ]  or  D-Pad Left / Right

Weapons

Fire turrets Left mouse button  or  RT2
Launch torpedo Lock a target, then fire the committed torpedo bay

The aim-assist lock is an angular cone: it grabs the hostile nearest your aim, and panning the view re-targets to whoever is now closest to center. Turrets draw their lead from the lock and paint an amber pip on the intercept point.

Interface

Cycle HUD (All → Minimal → None) ` (grave / tilde)
Pause menu Esc
Back to editor (Sandbox only) F1
New here? Hit ORBIT near the big rock and watch the ship fly itself into a clean circle - it is the fastest way to see the diegetic autopilot at work.
